Tips for Mindful Phone Use

Small changes can make a big difference

⏰ Set App Limits

Limit daily usage of social media to avoid endless scrolling.

📵 No Phone Zones

Keep phones away from dining table and study area for better focus.

🌙 Night Mode

Use blue light filter or night mode after dark to protect your eyes.

🛏️ Phone-Free Before Bed

Switch off screens 30 minutes before sleep to improve rest quality.

🔔 Turn Off Non-Essential Notifications

Silence distractions so you stay in control, not your phone.

📚 Learn, Don’t Just Scroll

Follow pages or channels that teach skills or inspire you.

💡 Track Your Screen Time

Review weekly reports to see where your time really goes.

👥 Connect in Real Life

Use your phone to meet friends offline, not just online.

✍️ Daily Reflection

Spend a minute each night thinking about how you used your phone today.

⚙️ Customize Your Home Screen

Place useful apps in front and hide distracting ones deeper.

Mindful use keeps your phone as a tool, not a trap.
